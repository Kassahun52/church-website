What We Offer

Our Services

Established in 1992, the St, Mary's Ethiopian Orthodox Church has been serving the Tampa Bay community and surr cities for over 34 Years Services include Sunday School, religious counseling, marriage, burial rites(Fitat), baptism, visiting and conforming the sick.

💧

Baptism / ጥምቀት

Sacrament of Christian initiation through water and the Holy Spirit. "Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost." — Matthew 28:19

Confession / ኑዛዜ
